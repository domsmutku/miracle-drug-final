Frequently asked questions

What exactly is The Miracle Drug™?
The Miracle Drug™ is an innovative wellness-support solution designed to empower employees to better navigate today’s fast-paced, outcome-driven work environment.

How will I know if The Miracle Drug™ is working?
Outcomes may vary by individual. Common indicators include reduced perception of personal distress, diminished curiosity and random bursts of euphoria. Success is best understood through observable behaviors and performance indicators rather than subjective experience.

Are there any side effects?
Some employees have reported mild effects such as memory loss, blue urine and minor hallucinations. These effects are generally temporary or become unnoticeable over time.
